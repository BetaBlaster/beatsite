What's The Beat?

Hailing from sunny La Jolla, CA, The Beat distinguishes itself by its values in working hard, playing hard, and - most importantly - loving each other to the max. This group of singers with diverse interests and backgrounds was brought together in 2004 by their passion for music - a passion that blossomed into an immense love and respect for one another.

From jazz to country to rock and pop, The Beat always delivers its own personal soul and style. The group has covered artists such as Sara Bareilles, Queen, Ed Sheeran, Beyonce, TLC, Justin Timberlake, Florence + the Machine, Nicki Minaj, and much more! In Fall of 2011, The Beat released their third CD entitled “Boom Baby!” ​ Now in its fifteenth year, The Beat has had the privilege to perform everywhere, including weddings, museums, senior citizen nursing homes, various campus events, and at the International Championship of Collegiate A Cappella (ICCA), winning first place at their quarterfinal in 2014 (then advancing to the West Semifinals), and third in 2015, along with various awards for choreography and arrangements. The group also hosts two main shows during the academic year: a joint concert in the fall that features other San Diegan a cappella groups and a themed concert/musical extravaganza in the spring! Past spring shows have been: “The ZomBeat Acapocalypse”, “Spongebob Squarebeat”, “Beat to the Future”, “Harry Potter and the Beat of the Yule Ball” and more!

​ Their unmistakable friendship shines through in each performance, making for an enchanting and moving experience. This is not just another talented group of singers, but a family.
